A class on programming and web development at Olin College, Spring 2012.

Meets Tuesdays/Fridays 3:20–5:00 in the Crescent Room. (Download an iCal)


  • Build legit web apps from the ground up. Apps which will make your resume burn bright with ethereal flame.
  • Learn web development and coding skills you need to kick butt at companies like Twitter, Facebook, Google, and Microsoft.
  • Meet and work with representatives from swingin' tech companies in the Boston area.
  • Four ENGR credits, which will satisfy your OSS requirement and your awesomeness requirement for the semester. You need this to graduate.

Tim Ryan
github/tcr
Tim was born, raised, and will one day die in the loving embrace of New England. He has been using the Internet since he was just four years old, sharpened his teeth on AOL webcomics and Invader Zim message boards, and made horrible abominations on Geocities using everyone's favorite language, JavaScript. Ten years later, this became the cool thing to do and Tim rocketed to Internet stardom. He's authored compilers, <canvas> shims, and trendy Lisps, developed for Intuit, PMed Microsoft, and rocked the HTML5 boat at Game Closure. He's interested in making computing more accessible to everyone, and has a penchant for APIs and platforms. Tim loves acting, vintage video games, and typography, and one day you'll find him mad, fleeing to the coast of Ireland, writing fishing novels.
